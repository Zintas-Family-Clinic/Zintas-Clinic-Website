Medications & Treatment

We prescribe FDA-approved GLP-1 medications including semaglutide (Wegovy®, Ozempic®) and tirzepatide (Zepbound®, Mounjaro®) based on clinical evaluation and your medical history. Your provider will determine the best option for you.

Medications are delivered to your home or sent to your preferred local pharmacy based on provider recommendations.

Common side effects include mild nausea, constipation, or digestive changes, which typically improve over time. Your provider will discuss all potential side effects during your consultation and provide guidance on managing them. We monitor you closely throughout treatment.

Telehealth Disclaimers

General Telehealth Disclaimer

Telehealth services allow you to receive medical care through secure video, phone, or electronic communication. By participating in a telehealth appointment, you acknowledge and understand that:

  • Telehealth is not a substitute for in-person medical care when a physical examination is required.
  • Your provider may determine that an in-person visit is necessary for diagnosis or treatment.
  • Technical issues such as poor internet connection or device malfunction may affect your session.
  • All telehealth sessions will follow privacy and confidentiality standards required by law (e.g., HIPAA), but no technology can guarantee 100% security.

⚠️ Emergency Services Disclaimer

Telehealth services are not for medical emergencies.

If you are experiencing a medical emergency—such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, loss of consciousness, or suicidal thoughts—call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department immediately.

Limitations of Telehealth

Telehealth has limitations, including:

  • Some conditions cannot be fully assessed without a physical exam, lab tests, imaging, or procedures.
  • Prescriptions and treatment plans may be limited based on your provider's judgment.
  • Not all medications can be prescribed through telehealth due to federal and state laws.

Financial and Billing Disclaimer

  • Telehealth visits may be billed to your insurance when applicable.
  • You are responsible for any copayments, deductibles, or non-covered services.
  • It is your responsibility to verify telehealth coverage with your insurance provider.

Privacy and Confidentiality Disclaimer

Your telehealth visit uses secure, encrypted technology. While your clinic takes all reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • There is a small risk of unauthorized access due to the nature of electronic communication.
  • You should participate from a private location where your personal information cannot be overheard or seen by others.
  • You are responsible for the privacy of your own device and internet connection.

By using our telehealth services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to these disclaimers.
